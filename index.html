Interactive Map of Student Aid

About the Map

This interactive map is based on the 2020 data from the Student Support and Fees Dataset (SSFD), initiated by the Swedish Institute for Social Research. It provides a comparative analysis of student financial support and tuition fees across countries.

The data is standardized for comparability over time and adjusted for inflation using the 2020 Implied PPP conversion rate from the International Monetary Fund.
